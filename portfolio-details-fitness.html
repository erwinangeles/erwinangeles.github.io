About this Project

This client started as a personal fitness coach and I helped them establish an online presence by creating a marketing page designed to take people interested in fitness through a fitness quiz. The quiz generates leads for the client, which they can then use to market or follow up with them about signing up for fitness training. The website also works to promote the client's mobile app, which is shown across all pages of the site. The main objective of this site is to push people to take the online quiz, and sign up as customers.
